Help & Support

We're here to help you get the most out of MaGame.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sign in with an email code?

On the login screen, enter your email address and tap Continue. We'll send you a one-time password (OTP) — a short code valid for 5 minutes. Type the code into the app and you're in. No passwords to remember.

Can I play as a guest, without an account?

Yes! Choose Continue as Guest on the login screen and start playing right away — no email needed. Keep in mind that guest progress is tied to your device and may be lost if you uninstall the app. Sign in with email anytime to keep your XP, levels and progress safe.

How do I create or join an online room?

To create a room: pick a game (Spy or Mafia), tap Create Room, and share the room code with your friends.

To join a room: tap Join Room and enter the code your friend shared. Everyone joins in seconds, and you can chat with other players in the room while you play.

How do hints and rewarded ads work?

Stuck on a solo puzzle? You can get a hint by choosing to watch a short rewarded video ad. Ads are always opt-in — we never show banners or interrupt your game. You decide when (and if) to watch one in exchange for a hint.

I'm not receiving my OTP code — what should I do?

First, check your spam or junk folder — the code arrives from our email service within a minute or two. Also double-check that you typed your email address correctly.

Note: for security, you can request a maximum of 3 codes per 15 minutes. If you've hit the limit, wait a few minutes and try again. Each code expires after 5 minutes.

How do I delete my account?

Email us at mahammadalipashayev@gmail.com with the subject "Account Deletion Request" from the email address linked to your account. We will delete your account and associated data within 30 days, as described in our Privacy Policy.

How do I change the language or theme?

Open Settings in the app. There you can switch the interface language and choose between light and dark themes — the change applies instantly.
